How We Manage Assets

At Swahilipot, we use a comprehensive system to manage all our assets efficiently. Hereâ€™s a brief overview of how we handle asset management:

  • Asset Registration: Every asset is registered in our system with details such as name, category, and condition. This helps us keep track of all assets from the moment they are acquired.
  • Asset Assignment: When an asset is assigned to an organization or department, it is logged in our system, including details like the quantity, organization name, and phone number. This allows us to monitor where each asset is used.
  • Asset Return: Upon returning an asset, its condition is assessed, and it is either added back to inventory or moved to maintenance if damaged. The system updates asset quantities and maintains records of returns.
  • Maintenance Management: Damaged assets are reported and moved to our maintenance list. Our maintenance team handles repairs and updates the asset status upon completion.
  • Reporting and Analysis: Regular reports and analyses are generated to ensure optimal asset usage and management. This helps in making informed decisions and maintaining asset health.
